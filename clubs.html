KABBADI

The game is played with 20-minute halves with a 5-minute half break in which the teams exchange sides. During each play, known as a "raid", a player from the attacking side, known as the "raider", runs into the opposing team's side of the court and attempts to tag as many of the sseven defending players as possible.

MALLAKHAMB

Mallakhamb is a traditional sport, originating from the Indian subcontinent, in which a gymnast performs aerial yoga or gymnastic postures and wrestling grips in concert with a vertical stationary or hanging wooden pole, cane, or rope. The word Mallakhamb also refers to the pole used in the sport.

VOLLEYBALL

Volleball is played with a ball and a net,with teams on each side of net.One team hits the ball over the net and into the other team's court,the other team must then hit the ball back over the net and in bounds within 3 tries without letting the ball touch the ground. "Team volleyball",(indoors)with 12 (6 per team) & Beach volleyball (outdoors) with 2 per team.

WRESTLING

Wrestling is a combat sport involving grappling-type techniques such as clinch fighting, throws and takedowns, joint locks, pins and other grappling holds. The sport can either be genuinely competitive or sportive entertainment (see professional wrestling).

SWIMMING

There are four strokes in competetive swimming.The freestyle has no restrictions.The three other style are the backstroke, the breaststroke which is commonly called as frog style and the butterfly.Freestyle races are majorly over 50m,100m,200m,400m and extra 1500m for men & extra 800m for women.

WEIGHTLIFTING

Olympic weightlifting, or Olympic-style weightlifting (officially named Weightlifting), is a sport in which athletes compete in lifting a barbell loaded with weight plates from the ground to overhead, with each athlete vying to successfully lift the heaviest weights
